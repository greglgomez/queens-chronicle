#### 1. Family Matters

#### Alchemic Preservation
During the Age of Disorder, in a cave outside Lybra, a woman's corpse was found—eerily preserved, with a sharp metallic odor. You, one of the kingdom's leading scholars, have been tasked with examining it. Her organs are stiff but intact. No blood remains, only a clear, pungent liquid with irritating properties flowing through her veins. If you can understand and replicate this substance, you might unlock an unkown—or long-forgotten—element of nature.
